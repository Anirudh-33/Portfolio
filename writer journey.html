JOURNEY AS A WRITER

Anirudh set foot in the art of writing at the age of fifteen. Having literature as a weak spot with average scores, yet determined enough he kept fine-tuning his writing style, gradually developing a unique way to express — through instances and references. Eventually discovering that writing was meant to be the part of his life journey, he adopted the art like a transfusion. Inspired by many, honoured Alan Wilson Watts, JK Rowling and George RR Martin remain the biggest influences on him. With the feeling instilled to impact others, he embarked on the journey to convey the peculiarity of art and significance of simplicity in life.

Upon discovering the way of simplicity, he realized life can be made happy and wholesome. He got in touch with spirituality and had in him the will to dive deeper knowing that life and reality are composed of mysteries and he had the urge to figure things out. As his this feeling was accompanied by the willingnesss to share his knowledge with others, his writing skill supported him to do so effectively.

His spiritual blog — Ink&Roses, and entertainment blog — Hot Chocolate, are two impeccable specimens of his creativity.
